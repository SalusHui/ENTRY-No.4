<h>ENTRY-No.4: Corrupted Fragments</h>

<p style="color: green;">Corrupted fragments are individuals of codes and scripts 
inhabited by corruptions, and unlike infected fragments, it is not only affected 
by the corruptions of subjects, the corruptions have taken full control of the 
individual, while still remaining some sentience, the individuals don't have control 
over themselves.

Corrupted fragments are rare to be made, but there are 5 that's trapped in the codes
of certain systems. A warning that corrupted fragments are incredibly dangerous, and
will try as aggressive as possible to infect the user's device when encountered.</p>
